PittMesh is a community wireless network in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, USA.

PittMesh is a wireless mesh network constructed under the auspices of non-profit Meta Mesh Wireless Communities. For a brief introduction to PittMesh and mesh networking in general, please view our informational video.

PittMesh currently has dozens of live sites comprised of many devices. There are a few planned sites. There are many point-to-point links between sites.

Want to help build the future of connectivity in Pittsburgh? Contact Meta Mesh. You can also tell us about your PittMesh node.
